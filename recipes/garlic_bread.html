Garlic Bread Recipe

Yummy Garlic Bread

The garlic bread is a delicious snack, a staple for a pizza place, or for you working at home!

This simple recipe only requires 7 ingredients, and it is quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 60cm baguette
  • 125g unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cloves of fresh garlic, minced
  • 2tsp fresh parsley, minced
  • 2 sausages, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, according to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C or 390F
  2. Cut the baguette into two halves, then cut to about 2cm width
  3. In a bowl, mix the melted better, garlic, salt, pepper, parsley, and minced sausage together
  4. Spread the mixture onto the baguette on both sides

Enjoy your garlic bread!
